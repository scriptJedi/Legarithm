Requirements

What are the requirements to register a company in Cyprus?

Corporate secretary

The law requires that every company has a secretary. This officer is responsible for compliance with legal requirements. He or she files annual returns and resolutions on changes, prepares the annual general meeting, and also deals with the distribution of dividends

Director/nominee director

At least one of your directors must be a resident. Hiring a nominee director is a common and legal practice. A nominee director puts his or her name on your documents but cannot make any key business decisions. In the event of any wrongdoing, such as late or incorrect reporting, the nominee director shares the legal responsibility. That is why you should get an accounting package with this service.

Legal address

All companies must have a local registered address. It is indicated on all legal documents. We receive your correspondence, scan it and forward it to you.
